About

Reading

Jordan is an avid reader and often enjoys doing so on her morning commute to work. She’s partial to books with strong female protagonists that have been made into TV series, such as “Handmaid's Tale”, “Big Little Lies”, “Sharp Objects”, and others.

Fashion

Having studied costume design in school, Jordan has always enjoyed clothing and fashion. She’d often sneak the latest styles in her backpack to school, and change in the bathroom to avoid the scowl of her mother.

Socializing

Being blessed with the “gift of the gab” Jordan loves going out with her friends and meeting new ones. She’s not a nosy busybody, but she enjoys “spilling the tea” on occasion. She is inspired by the girls she’s met in the bathrooms at bars, and collectively wishes them all well.
